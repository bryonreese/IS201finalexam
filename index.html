About

A little bit about me

Family

I am in a family of 5 with two older sisters. I love my family. My mom has 4 siblings, and my dad was an only child. My family likes to go on walks and hikes together to spend quality time. Me and my dad will go golfing. My family really likes to find new restaurants that we haven't had or heard of before.

Sports

I love all sports, I play a lot of Basketball, Pickleball, Football, and I love going to the gym. I am a big fan of all BYU sports, the Philadelphia Eagles, the Utah Jazz, and the San Francisco 49ers. I like the Jazz because I am from Utah, my dad got offered a job with the Eagles, and I am going to move to the Bay Area so I like the 49ers.

Finance

Coming into college I really enjoyed statistics, but realized my true passion is Finance. I am going to work in Investment Banking and hoping to transition to Private Equity. My sister studied finance and was a big help in me deciding to switch as well as some Finance Professors here at BYU. I have loved the program so far and look forward to a career in Finance.

Education

My Educational Background

    Maple Mountain High School

    I graduated High School from Maple Mountain with a 3.95 GPA. I scored a 25 on the ACT and played on the soccer team. I also wrestled for the Freshman team and was the state champion for Rec-League, or 1st-3rd year wrestlers.

  • August 2022 - April 2026

    Brigham Young University

    I am a student at Brigham Young University studying Finance. I am currently a sophomore with an expected graduation date of 2026. I am involved in the Investment Banking Association, and the Private Equity Venture Capital Association as well.

My professional experience

    Awardco

    Banyan Growth Partners
