I love investing

There are various types of industries and sub-industries in which you can invest.

  1. Software
    • CyberSecurity - Companies like Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, and F5 ahve proven how great Cyber Security companies can be. The government has great incentive to ensure that Cyber Security companies continue to perform as it is a national threat as well.
    • CRM companies - CRM tools are essential for companies to survive and provide support for all of their clients needs. Monday is a great CRM tool, as well as SalesForce.
  2. Hardware
    • GPU manufacturers - These companies manufacture GPUs, which are Graphic Processing Units. They are most used for Video Games and large Data Centers.
    • CPU manufacturers - These companies manufacture CPUs, which are Central Processing Units are used for basic computing, analyzing big data, etc.
  3. Consumer
    • Retail companies - These companies will sell you clothes, or everyday items. They will be around for a long time because everyone always needs stuff. Hard to find clear market leaders in this space.
    • Experience/Entertainment Companies - These are companies who sell you more of an experience rather than an item. Think TopGolf, Amusement Parks, Aquariums, Zoos, etc.
